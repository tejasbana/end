About Me

I'm a final year student at DYPCOE, Pune University, pursuing B.E in Information Technology. I am broadly interested in Generative Models, privacy-preserving machine learning, Fully homomorphic encryption, and Differential Privacy.

Research Experience: I am working at IIT Bombay on the Virtual Tryon and similarity learning for fashion items using Vision Transformer. Before that, I spent a couple of months working with the Indian Army to create a dynamic camouflage pattern generator software. The project has received a lot of attention and is under final testing.

Industry Experience: As a data science intern at Scaler Academy, I write articles and set questions on statistics, data preprocessing, and deep learning. Before that, I worked with Architects from Uniworks Designs to automate the process of designing architectural floor plans using graph-constrained generative adversarial networks.
